Post 1: Tomorrow Comes

By: Alex Kempken Date: 6/23/2025

So today I just finished Clair Obscur, and overall I absolutely loved playing the game. I really enjoyed the gameplay even though the parry mechanics were sometimes frame perfect timing, but I'm sure there will be an update at some point to adjust that. The story itself was absolutely beautiful with some parts moving me close to crying. The bosses also were some of my favorite. Of course I can't forget about the music of the game. The music was so good thats its hard to imagine that this was Lorien Toustard's first video game score. My favorite out of all them had to be "Une vie a t'aime" from the fight against Renoir.

Post 2: We Lost

By: Alex Kempken Date: 6/28/2025

Ok it's been three days since I finished the game and I'm still playing Clair Obscur. Why? Because this optional boss fight is an ABSOLUTE NIGHTMARE. While finishing up the rest of the endgame content, I ended up coming across a high level area in the ocean known as "Renoir's Drafts" which contained a lot of the final enemies from the endgame. That however wasn't the issue, it is the boss that appears at the very end of it all, Simon. This boss is so ridiculously hard that I am still struggling three days late to actually finish it. Worse part is that just when I think i finally beat him and his 24 million health pool, a cutscene reveal he has a phase 2, which has triple the health, and his attack now have double the strikes I need to dodge and parry.
